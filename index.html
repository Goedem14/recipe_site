Recipe

Chocolate cake



Here is a fast and easy version of the classis Chochlate Cake recipe. Enjoy !




Difficulty Prepation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

  • Black Chochlate: 200g
  • Sugar: 150g
  • Butter: 200g
  • Eggs: 4
  • Flour: 80g
  • Much of Love



Instructions

  1. Melt the Chochlate and the Butter together
  2. Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour
  3. Mix everything to get a smooth dough
  4. Bake in the oven
